WINES OF ITALY

Italy is a land of wine. From the hilly and majestic landscapes of
Piedmont in the north come: Barolo, Barbera, Barbaresco, Freisa and
Asti Spumante.

Frecciarossa comes from Lombardy to the south of Lake Garda;
Valpolicella and Soave from Verona in the province of Venetia; Chianti
from Tuscany, rich for its world famous art treasures; Orvieto from
Umbria; Capri from near Naples and Lacrima Christi grown on the
southern volcanic slopes of Mount Vesuvius, come from the picturesque
fertile lands of Campania.

Many other vineyards having claim to fame stud the beautiful
peninsula.
